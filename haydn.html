Franz Joseph Haydn

Country of Origin: Austria

Years of Life: March 1732 - May 1809

Musical Era: Classical

About: Franz Joseph Haydn was born in Rohrau Austria in 1732. While his family could not read music, they were extremely musical and noticed a talent in young Haydn at an early age. Haydn was sent to study music with a choirmaster in Hainburg at the age of 6. He learned harpsichord and violin and sang in the choir. As he grew older, Haydn performed, taught and composed.
